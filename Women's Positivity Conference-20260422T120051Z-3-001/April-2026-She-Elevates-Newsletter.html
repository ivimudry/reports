Welcome to the She Elevates Team, Christina Miner!

We are so excited to introduce Christina Miner as DeLores's new Executive Assistant! Christina joins She Elevates with a remarkable background as the Founder of Women's Positivity Conferences, Retreats & Events, where she has built spaces for women to connect, feel inspired, and lift one another up.

A graduate of Cuyahoga Falls High School and The University of Akron, Christina is a former President and Board Member of the Cuyahoga Falls Chamber of Commerce, a dedicated community volunteer, and a breast cancer survivor. Her journey through advanced breast cancer, chemotherapy, and multiple surgeries shaped a deep gratitude for life and a powerful belief in faith, perspective, and encouragement — values that inspired her to build a community focused on uplifting and empowering women.

A wife, mom, daughter, sister, and friend, Christina loves the outdoors, walks, pickleball, travel, helping others, and live music — especially her husband's band. We could not be happier to welcome her energy and heart to the She Elevates team.

Registration Still Open: 2026 She Elevates Academy

Have you registered your daughter for the 2026 She Elevates Academy yet? Our free summer program is designed for girls ages 8–14 and is built to help them grow in confidence, strengthen leadership skills, and explore the fundamentals of business ownership in a fun, encouraging environment.

Throughout the week, participants take part in engaging lessons and hands-on experiences that encourage them to think bigger, believe in themselves, and discover new possibilities for their future. No business experience needed — just curiosity, courage, and a willingness to learn.

The Academy is open to participants in Stark County and surrounding areas, and we encourage all families to apply before the June 12 deadline.

📍Location: One Center for Leadership, 901 Tuscarawas Street E., Canton, Ohio 44707
📅Dates: July 13–17, 2026
⏰Time: 8:00 AM – 3:30 PM
🎓Graduation: July 18, 2026

DeLores Featured at the Fourth Annual Women's Positivity Conference

On April 17, 2026, our founder DeLores took the stage as a guest speaker and featured author at the Fourth Annual Women's Positivity Conference, held at the Sheraton Suites in Cuyahoga Falls, OH.

The day brought together inspiring women from across the region for a celebration of empowerment, leadership, and shared stories. DeLores shared her journey and the mission behind She Elevates — encouraging the next generation of girls to step boldly into their own potential. We're so proud to see her voice and message lifted up alongside such an incredible community of women.
