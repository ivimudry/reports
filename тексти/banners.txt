General requirements
Email banners
Size: 600x300 px


Use Pantherbet brand colors


CTA button:


CLAIM BONUS - for offer-based emails


PLAY NOW - for non-offer emails


Visual direction:


use the brand panther character


if the email is about Free Spins, use the game character integrated naturally into the Pantherbet visual style


Do not mention the game title on the banner


Banner text should be short, CTA-style, and promo-driven


Do not include conditions like deposit ranges, minimum deposit, “up to,” or similar details


Do not include promo codes in the banner text
