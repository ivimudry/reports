Welcome to the She Elevates Team, Christina Miner!

Please welcome Christina Miner, who joins She Elevates this month as DeLores's new Executive Assistant. Christina is the founder of Women's Positivity Conferences, Retreats & Events — a community built to give women a place to connect and support each other.

She's a graduate of Cuyahoga Falls High School and The University of Akron, a former President of the Cuyahoga Falls Chamber of Commerce, and a breast cancer survivor. Coming through that experience left her with a deeper sense of gratitude and a steady belief that perspective and encouragement matter — even on the hard days.

Outside of work she's a wife and mom who loves long walks, pickleball, travel, and live music — especially when her husband's band is playing. We're glad to have her with us.

Has your daughter signed up for the 2026 She Elevates Academy? It's our free summer program for girls ages 8–14, focused on confidence, leadership, and the basics of running a business — in a way that's actually fun.

No prior business knowledge needed — just curiosity. Open to families in Stark County and surrounding areas. Applications close June 12, so don't wait.

We're excited to highlight recent updates and news from She Elevates!

On April 17, 2026, our founder DeLores spoke as a guest speaker and featured author at the Fourth Annual Women's Positivity Conference at the Sheraton Suites in Cuyahoga Falls, OH.

She talked about the road that led to She Elevates and why this work matters — building real opportunities for the next generation of girls. A powerful day, and we're proud her story was part of it.
