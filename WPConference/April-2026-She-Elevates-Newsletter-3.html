Welcome to the She Elevates Team, Christina Miner!

Please join us in welcoming Christina Miner, who joins She Elevates this month as DeLores's new Executive Assistant. Christina is the founder of Women's Positivity Conferences, Retreats & Events — a community she built to give women a place to connect, feel seen, and support each other.

A graduate of Cuyahoga Falls High School and The University of Akron, Christina is a former President and Board Member of the Cuyahoga Falls Chamber of Commerce, a longtime community volunteer, and a breast cancer survivor. Coming through advanced breast cancer, chemotherapy, and several surgeries gave her a deeper sense of gratitude and a steadier belief that perspective and encouragement matter — even on the hard days. That is the spirit she brings to her work uplifting other women, and now to her work with us.

Outside of work, Christina is a wife, mom, daughter, sister, and friend who likes long walks, pickleball, travel, and live music — especially when her husband's band is playing. We're glad to have her with us.

Has your daughter signed up for the 2026 She Elevates Academy? It's our free summer program for girls ages 8–14, focused on confidence, leadership, and the basics of running a business — taught in a way that's actually fun.

Across the week, the girls work through hands-on lessons and activities that push them to think bigger about what they're capable of. No prior business knowledge needed — just curiosity and a willingness to try.

The Academy is open to families in Stark County and the surrounding areas. Applications close June 12, so don't wait too long.

We're excited to highlight recent updates and news from She Elevates!

On April 17, 2026, our founder DeLores joined the lineup as a guest speaker and featured author at the Fourth Annual Women's Positivity Conference at the Sheraton Suites in Cuyahoga Falls, OH.

She talked about the road that led to She Elevates and why this work matters — building real opportunities for the next generation of girls. It was a powerful day, and we're proud DeLores got to share her story with that room.
